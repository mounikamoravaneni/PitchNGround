# PitchNGround
Book nearby sports grounds &amp; trainers easily. Pay &amp; play in your city

#Description
PitchNGround is your all-in-one app to book nearby sports grounds, discover trainers, and reserve your favorite sports slots with ease!

 Nearby Grounds Based on Location
Quickly find available sports grounds within 15 km of your current location or search by area. Whether it’s football, cricket, badminton, or tennis – we’ve got you covered.

Slot Booking & Real-Time Availability
View ground details, available time slots, and reserved slots in real-time. Book your preferred ground and time instantly.

 Cashfree Secure Payments
Enjoy secure, fast payments through Cashfree. Cancel easily within 24 hours for a full refund.

 Trainer Discovery & Profiles
Explore verified trainer profiles, show interest in them, and get started with personalized sports coaching.

 Redeem Points & Promocodes
Use promocodes and redeem points to get exciting discounts while booking.
